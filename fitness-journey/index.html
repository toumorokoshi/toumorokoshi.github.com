Yusuke Tsutsumi's Fitness Journey

In 2022, I started an earnest effort to improve my fitness. This documents that journey.

These are mostly personal notes, but why not share?

2025-01-09

Took a Dexa scan today, with the updates being:

  • body fat 13.0% -> 12.1% (lowest since scanning!). 19.2lb fat loss total. Highest amount lost since measuring.
  • total mass down 2.8lbs.
  • fat mass down 1.9lbs, lowest level.
  • lean mass down 0.8lbs, up 4.4lbs from peak.
  • Fat mass is down across the board, except for Gynoid.
  • Visceral adipose tissue is unchanged - 0.35lbs. Up 0.32 from best, down 0.23 from worst.
  • Muscle mass is up from baseline, but down from peak.
    • leg lifts helped build my calfs up significantly. This, combined with possibly some squats / deadlifts, have upped my legs muscle by 3lbs from last time.
    • Trunk is down 4.5lbs from peak. -1.1 from baseline.
    • Android is down -0.2 from baseline. But very negligable.
    • Gynoid is down 1lb from .baseline, 3lbs from peak.

Next steps:

  • Focus primarily on building muscle. I think my body fat won’t go down that much anymore. Maybe continue a minor deficit.
  • Continue Leg, Arms as is. They are responsive to the current workout.
  • Add workouts specifically for Trunk, Gynoid, and android.
    • Trunk is everything aside than head, neck, limbs.
    • Android / Gynoid are subcomponents of trunk
    • Gynoid is hips / thighs (upper thighs, are a part of it).
    • Android must be abs / chest.
  • From Dexa guy:
    • Squats / core exercises
    • Targetting of glutes.
  • Looking at the visual dex scans, my upper legs have actually become much thinner. So I need to focus on targetting exercise in the upper legs (quads)

2024-07-17

I took a two-week vacation in Japan in June, where I wasn’t working out for basically weeks straight, which probably impacted my lean mass. My Dexa scan today revealed:

  • Increased body fat from 12.3% -> 13.0%.
  • Decreased lean mass from 149.7 -> 147.4lbs
    • Leg muscle: -2.8lb (1.2 lost in gynoid)
    • Trunk: +0.5lb
  • Total mass down from 178.1 -> 176.7

So basically I gained fat, lost muscle.

My Visceral Adipose Tissue also went back up, from 0.03lbs to 0.35bs. So take aways are:

  • Interestingly, VAT was higher than it was when I had 15% body fat. Maybe that’s a margin of error?
  • To bring my VAT down to 0, I need less than 12.3% body fat.
  • to bring my VAT down to 0, I need less than 12.3% body fat. Probably 10%?

Next Steps:

  • Target 10% body fat - This means losing 5 lbs of fat, assuming lean mass is constant (which is likely, given the fat and muscle loss). So 171lbs.
  • Focus on increasing leg mass (basically more leg workouts)
  • Change my workout regime to work on building muscle, vs a mix of Crossfit, random cardio in the middle.
  • Continue a caloric deficit. I think I’m officially entering a “cutting” phase and once desired body fat is reached, I’ll start a “bulking” phase.
