Yusuke Tsutsumi's Fitness Journey

In 2022, I started an earnest effort to improve my fitness. This documents that journey.

These are mostly personal notes, but why not share?

2024-07-17

I took a two-week vacation in Japan in June, where I wasn’t working out for basically weeks straight, which probably impacted my lean mass. My Dexa scan today revealed:

  • Increased body fat from 12.3% -> 13.0%.
  • Decreased lean mass from 149.7 -> 147.4lbs
    • Leg muscle: -2.8lb (1.2 lost in gynoid)
    • Trunk: +0.5lb
  • Total mass down from 178.1 -> 176.7

So basically I gained fat, lost muscle.

My Visceral Adipose Tissue also went back up, from 0.03lbs to 0.35bs. So take aways are:

  • Interestingly, VAT was higher than it was when I had 15% body fat. Maybe that’s a margin of error?
  • To bring my VAT down to 0, I need less than 12.3% body fat.
  • to bring my VAT down to 0, I need less than 12.3% body fat. Probably 10%?

Next Steps:

  • Target 10% body fat - This means losing 5 lbs of fat, assuming lean mass is constant (which is likely, given the fat and muscle loss). So 171lbs.
  • Focus on increasing leg mass (basically more leg workouts)
  • Change my workout regime to work on building muscle, vs a mix of Crossfit, random cardio in the middle.
  • Continue a caloric deficit. I think I’m officially entering a “cutting” phase and once desired body fat is reached, I’ll start a “bulking” phase.
