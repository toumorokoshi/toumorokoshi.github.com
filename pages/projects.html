Slimy: HTML5 Slideshow

Slimy is an HTML5 slideshow designed for simplicity. Basically, it was built off the principles of simple and quick creation of slideshows, utilizing the most recent HTML5 and CSS3 standards, and lack of onlide dependencies. These ensure a very robust, universal and experience with offline compatability. You can see an example of slimy in action here

3D Holographic Projector

My final project for my undergraduate degrees, the 3D Hologpraphic projector generates a holographic image by reflecting an image off of a spinning, angled mirror, changing images rapidly to project different images to different angles, effectively creating a three-dimensional, holographic image. The design is based off of the one a group from USC created. In addition the projector, an interface was also created to interface with the 3D object, utilizing the Microsoft Kinect.
